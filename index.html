Overview

At the 2024 Child Nutrition Access and Accountability through Technology (CNAAT) conference, I led the full strategy, execution, and promotion of PrimeroEdge's presence—driving the brand's largest reception yet and exceeding lead generation goals. This out-of-state event required cross-functional alignment, real-time logistics management, and strict adherence to USDA branding regulations.
